# Outreach Plan for Full-Stack Engineer Position

## Objective
Share the job posting for Full-Stack Engineer (TypeScript/Python) on relevant platforms to attract qualified candidates.

## Platforms
1. LinkedIn Jobs
2. Indeed
3. Stack Overflow Jobs
4. GitHub Jobs
5. RemoteOK
6. We Work Remotely
7. AngelList (for startup roles)
8. Twitter/X (using relevant hashtags: #TypeScript, #Python, #VSCodeExtension, #AI)
9. Relevant Discord/Slack communities (e.g., VS Code extensibility, AI devs)
10. University career boards (if targeting recent grads)

## Outreach Schedule
- Week 1: Post on LinkedIn, Indeed, Stack Overflow, GitHub Jobs
- Week 2: Post on RemoteOK, We Work Remotely, AngelList, Twitter
- Week 3: Engage in communities, share in relevant forums
- Week 4: Review applications, start screening

## Tracking
We will maintain a simple tracker (CSV or spreadsheet) with:
- Date posted
- Platform
- Link to post
- Number of views/applications (if available)
- Notes

## Responsibility
CEO (Founder) will perform the initial outreach and track responses. Once applications are received, we will move to screening and interviewing.

## Notes
- Tailor the message slightly for each platform while keeping the core job description.
- Highlight the founding equity and impact.
- Mention the AI-enhanced IDE vision.

## Next Steps After Outreach
1. Review incoming applications.
2. Schedule screening calls.
3. Conduct technical interviews.
4. Make an offer.
